## Editor input

Same baseline as A2-WON. Only diff: **Word Wrap = OFF**.

## Wire summary

Same packet shape. NMG write UDP len 240 (vs A2-WON 241, A1 218). NMG payload 0xc8 = **200 B**.

## NMG body (200 B) — diff vs A2-WON

```
A2-WON: ... 1b 30 62 08 ... 07 30 W R A P T E S T 20 W I D E 0d 20 L I N E 20 1 2 3 4 5 0d 04 ...
A2-WOFF: ... 1b 30 61 08 ... 07 30 W R A P T E S T 20 W I D E 20 L I N E 20 1 2 3 4 5 0d 04 ...
```

### Wrap encoding confirmed

| Editor setting | Byte after `1b 30` |
|---|---|
| Wrap ON | `62 08` (`b` + `\x08`) |
| Wrap OFF | `61 08` (`a` + `\x08`) |

`62`/`61` is the wrap selector, `08` looks like a fixed companion byte (purpose TBD).

### Inline row break confirmed

Wrap ON injects `0d 20` mid-text at the line-break point. Wrap OFF emits the text contiguous, single trailing `0d 04`. Length diff 201 → 200 = exactly the missing `0d`. The leading-space artefact in wrap ON ate one byte too — net diff is the `0d` row separator alone after counting padding.

## Slot1 reading across A-set so far

| Capture | NMG len | Wrap | Slot1 dec | Slot1 hex |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A1 (`HI`) | 178 | ON (irrelevant) | 3000 | 0x0bb8 |
| A2-WON | 201 | ON | 8224 | 0x2020 |
| A2-WOFF | 200 | OFF | 29300 | 0x7274 |

Not a CRC. Not linear in NMG length. **Likely a display/dwell duration** in some unit:
- A1 fitted, 1 page, no scroll → 3 s plausible for Pause 2s + In/Out
- A2-WON fitted 2 wrapped pages → ~8.2 s (≈ 2 page dwells)
- A2-WOFF wrap off + overflow → text scrolls; ~29.3 s for one full scroll loop at medium speed seems plausible for the extra-wide line

If slot1 is total per-entry display time in ms, formula = pages × (pause + transitions) for fitted, scroll_distance / pixels-per-tick × tick-ms for marquee. Will confirm by varying Pause first.

## CRC16 finding (bonus)

The 56-byte UDP packet sent immediately after the NMG write is a **CRC validate** message. Its last 4 bytes are the CRC16-X25 of the NMG body (no preamble, full padded length).

| Capture | X25(NMG body) | matches CRC packet trailer |
|---|---|---|
| A1 | 0x7FE8 | ✓ `7f e8 00 00` |
| A2-WON | 0xAF6A | ✓ `af 6a 00 00` |
| A2-WOFF | 0x0031 | ✓ `00 31 00 00` |
| A4-WON | 0xEC0B | ✓ `ec 0b 00 00` |
| A4-WOFF | 0x7E14 | ✓ `7e 14 00 00` |

CRC16-X25 = poly 0x1021, init 0xFFFF, reflected, xorout 0xFFFF. Stored **BE16** on wire.
Domain = NMG body from `01 5a 30 30` magic for `slot2` (= NMG_len) bytes (excludes leading `01 00 01 00` outer-descriptor prefix).

This nails the "sigmaX25" already in `SigmaClient.swift` as the protocol's content CRC. Patches to `replayCapturedPackets` and `sendNmg` should reuse this.
